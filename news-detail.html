Index Rally as Tech Leads Gains; Investors Eye Earnings Season

Major indices advanced on Tuesday, led by large-cap technology names after several firms issued upbeat guidance. Gains were broad-based but most pronounced in growth segments, with momentum supported by improved liquidity conditions.

Investors are closely watching earnings season to validate current valuations. Early reports have shown resilient demand in cloud and AI-related services, while consumer spending appears mixed across categories.

On the macro front, inflation indicators continued to cool modestly, and the policy outlook remains steady. Currency markets stabilized after guidance from central banks underscored commitment to price stability.

Sector rotation into defensives like consumer staples suggests positioning for potential volatility ahead. However, constructive outlooks among analysts point to ongoing opportunities for selective exposure.
